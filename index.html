ABOUT US

Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Shiv RMC and Shiv Infra are committed to delivering top-quality construction solutions. Established in 2022, Shiv RMC specializes in ready-mix concrete, offering reliable and timely services with state-of-the-art equipment.

Shiv Infra focuses on advanced diaphragm wall construction, bringing innovative techniques and modern machinery to foundation systems. Together, we strive to build stronger, sustainable, and efficient infrastructure for our clients.
